Public SSH Pub Key Uploader

Dear learner,
SSH key uploader helps you access your servers after you lose your public key.
Basically, it just uploads your new public key to your server(s), so you wont need to request new servers.

It does not give you access after blocking yourself from your server(s) using a firewall.
For that, request new servers and set them up as guided in the projects; you won't need to use this.

Now, if after reading that you still don't understand what this aims to achieve, I encourage you not to use this app. Instead, read how SSH works and redo the projects to have everything in order.

Feel free to add IPs in any order or add one, two or all IPs depending on the server(s) you need to upload the pub key for
